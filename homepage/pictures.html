Pictures

This is the front of the building,

the side that the expressway is facing,

where Toys R Us and Party City were once located

before going out of business.

This is the back of the building,

where the entrance of the Multiplex Cinemas was once located

before it shut down.

This is how Triangle Plaza looks now.

A Tesla facility instead of an entertainment center

that everyone could enjoy, no matter their age.

They decided to keep the original architecture.

All they did was give the building a paint job

and take down the old signs.
